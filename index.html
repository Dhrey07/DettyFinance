What we offer

Our functionalities include but not limited to;

Loan Aggregation

Connect various loan accounts to the app, which will display all of the relevant information on a single dashboard.

Budgeting Tool

A budgeting tool that helps users track their expenses and identify areas where they can save money.

Debt Analysis

Analyze user's loan accounts to predict the repayment dates, amounts, and interests for each loan.

Income Analysis

Analyse and provide suggestions for ways to increase income or reduce expenses.

Marketplace

Sell debts to interested parties in a marketplace within the app.

Financial Education

Provides information and tips on managing debt, improving credit score and achieving financial goals.
